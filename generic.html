Founders

Taki Noda

Taki is the leader of our group. "He is the soul of street dancing". Taki is a town boy from Japan but he is a very experienced dancer. He started his dancing career since he wass really young. Started practicing street dance with his father at the age of 5. He firstly danced on the stage when he was 7, alone! Also, he won 3 prizes alone this year. His talent and hard working on street dancing make him one of the most famous boy in his town. Since 2014, he arrived in Melbourne and started his uni life, he and his 3 friends made the desicion to found the Cohesion club. Click the button below to read his full introduction.

Lara Wong

"Lara is a typical chinese but with unique ambitious" Taki says. Lara is from Beijing, China. She even didnt hear of Hiphop before she was 14. But at the age of 15, her first year of high school, she was amazed by the performance of a street dance club on her New student party. Ever since then she made her desicion to join one of the street dance club and started learning. when she was 18, she met Taki, Emma and Ben. The same love of hiphop makes them found Cohesion. Emma Leong

Emma is from Singapore. Her parents are all Ballet teachers. Ever since she is a liitl child, she started watching her parents teaching their students. She deeply understood that dancing is not a easy job. However, she realized the true beauty of dancing as well. She made up her mind to learn a kind of dance, though her parents strongly recommend Ballet, eventually, she chose. "I saw strong energy of hiphop, which ballet doesnt have". In 2014, she moved to melbourne with her parents. That the time she meet Taki, Ben and Lara. A journey of hiphop begins. Click the button below to read her full introduction.

Ben Lee

Ben is a Geelong boy. His father used to work in the local theatre. Therefor, when he was young, his mom always took him to theatre to visit his dad, where he watched plenty of dancing performance. "It is a life long impression to me". With the help of his father's teacher friend in the theatre, he started learning his favorate: street dance with him twice a week. Interest dradually became the habit. He kept going to watch a dance performance in the theatre once a week until he went to melbourne for his uni studying.
