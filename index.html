join a hip-hop battle?

"I believe anyone watched the show will be amazed..."
Welcome to Cohesion street dance club, where we both love and make hip hop! Cohesion has a strong passion to perform the most extraordinary street dances to all the audience, especially you, our youth. The aims and objectives of Cohesion are to make the street dance, as a kind of formal show, to be accepted by people as many as possible, and we want to invite all the youth who are interested on hip hop to join the Cohesion family, together we make changes to hip hop.
Though, we are only the boys and girls with the same hobby, we make Cohesion special. As long as you are a little keen on hip hop, come and watch the show, and make street dance stamped on your heart.
