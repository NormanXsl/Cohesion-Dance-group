Join us

Started in 2015, the Cohesion fourteen members have walked together for four years. We realized many audiences wishes to join us after they watch our shows. Therefore, it is time to make the Cohesion a bigger family. This year we have a new plan of recruiting a lot new members to join us.No matter how much you know about street dance, no matter whether you have learnt it before, no matter what is your nationality, no matter what's your gender, no matter what's your age. Only if you have a pure hobby of street dance, we are here waiting for you.
Two options for you to let us know your interest: fulfill the form below Or contact us according to the information we provide below. We hope to see you in the group.
